Handcrafted, Made in USA

For spring 2014, we decided to introduce a collection of dual tone moccasins using cream (our favorite color of the season) and a more vivid color to give some contrast, drama and interest. For this one, we chose to combine the cream with turquoise - our company color. The turquoise leather is a thick bullhide which offers stability and durability; the cream-colored leather is a glovetan - a type of full grain leather that's incredibly supple yet sturdy. We named this color combination "Crema Turquoise" and it's a part of our Contempo line of women's Cota moccasins. This canoe-style moccasin is a classic and it offers a great barefoot feel. These are perfect for year round use in warmer climates and spring through fall use in northern climates. We suggest wearing them with just about anything - jeans, khakis, capris, leggings, shorts, or even short skirts; in fact they even look great with swimwear or cover-ups at the beach, pool, or on the pontoon!

          Handcrafted, Made in USA

          Every year we get requests from our customers to expand our selection of premium moccasins - those made from game hides such as elk, buffalo and even moose. There are several benefits to using these premium hides: They're incredibly durable, they're incredibly comfortable (the elk, especially), and they feel like they're broken in the first time you put them on - with no painful binding or pinching. This new introduction, part of our "Select" line of moccasins, is called "Cacao," and it's the slightly darker successor of our highly successful "Hot Chocolate," which was a part of our line for the past two years; in fact, these are about as dark a brown as you'll find - and there's absolutely no hint of a reddish tint. The sole is made of a thick buffalo hide; the upper is made of the most soft/supple elk in a coordinating color. This canoe-style moccasin is a classic and it offers a great barefoot feel. These can be worn year round in warmer climates and they are perfect for spring through fall in northern climates. We suggest wearing them with just about anything, including jeans, capris, khakis, leggings or short skirts. These undoubtedly will end up being your most comfortable pair of footwear ever!
